Jimma Region is one of the two primary CoffeeLand pilot sites in Ethiopia.

- **Partner institution**: Jimma University, College of Agriculture & Veterinary Medicine
- **Landscape type**: Forest, Semi-forest, Homegarden and shaded-plantation coffee-forest systems
- **Status**: Field plots under establishment (2026 Q2-Q3)
- **Focus**: Shade tree diversity, soil health and coffee yield and quality, carbon stock, and AI-based mapping of extent and structure
