We are pleased to announce that the CoffeeLand kick-off meeting has been successfully concluded and the project has now officially started.

The formal kick-off was held in a hybrid format on Friday, 24 April 2026.

Moderated by Dr Belachew Gizachew the meeting included opening remarks from the partner institutions and a short presentation by Eva Johnson on project finances and disbursement, followed by the introduction and launch of the CoffeeLand project website.

The kick-off closed with final remarks and confirmation that project activities are now underway.
