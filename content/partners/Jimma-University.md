Jimma University is one of Ethiopia's leading public universities, recognized for its academic excellence, research strength, and enduring commitment to national development. Formed in the 1980s through the merger of the Jimma Institute of Health Sciences and the Jimma College of Agriculture, Jimma University is grounded in a strong legacy of excellence, innovation, and service. This foundation enabled the emergence of a comprehensive, multidisciplinary, and development-oriented university with a clear mission to address Ethiopia's evolving societal needs.

Despite its location outside the capital, Jimma University has consistently maintained a standard of academic excellence that distinguishes it among institutions across the country. Over the decades, it has grown into a vibrant center of intellectual inquiry, innovation, and professional training, attracting scholars, researchers, educators, and thought leaders from across Ethiopia and beyond. Widely recognized among the nation's top public universities, Jimma University's reputation is built on the quality, relevance, and impact of its academic programs and research contributions.

## International Collaboration

Jimma University has a long and distinguished history of international collaboration that continues to shape its identity as a globally engaged institution. Its roots in global partnership date back to the 1960s, when the Jimma College of Agriculture was established in collaboration with the University of Oklahoma in the United States. Since then, the University has cultivated enduring partnerships with academic institutions, development agencies, and research organizations around the world.

Over the years, Jimma University has hosted international faculty, researchers, and development professionals from India, the Philippines, Cuba, the United States, Europe, South Korea, Japan, and China, among others. These partnerships have significantly enriched the University's academic environment and research culture. International collaborators have contributed to high-impact scientific publications, globally relevant research, doctoral training, and interdisciplinary innovation. Today, Jimma University maintains numerous Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with institutions and organizations across Africa, Europe, the Middle East, Asia, Latin America, and North America, reflecting its strong commitment to diverse, mutually beneficial, and globally relevant partnerships.

As Jimma University continues through a transformative phase of institutional growth, it remains firmly committed to strengthening its academic excellence, expanding its global partnerships, and advancing impactful research and innovation. With sustained investment in infrastructure, human capital, and international collaboration, Jimma University is well positioned to further its vision of becoming a globally competitive and collaborative center of excellence in higher education, research, and community engagement.
