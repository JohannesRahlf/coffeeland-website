#### NIBIO -- Norwegian Institute of Bioeconomy Research

- **Role**: Project owner and lead research partner
- **Location**: Ås, Norway
- **Website**: [www.nibio.no](https://www.nibio.no)
- **Contribution**: Project management and coordination, AI/ML remote sensing, biomass/carbon mapping, 

## Institutional Information

- **Institution (Norwegian name)**: NIBIO - NORSK INSTITUTT FOR BIOØKONOMI
- **Address**: Postboks 115, 1431 Ås, Norway
- **General email**: [post@nibio.no](mailto:post@nibio.no)

## CoffeeLand Responsibilities

As project owner, NIBIO is responsible for:

- Overall project management and coordination
- Scientific leadership across work packages
- Development and integration of AI/ML methods for mapping coffee-forest systems
- Carbon stock modelling and monitoring methodology development
- Data management and open science implementation
- Financial and technical reporting to the Research Council of Norway
